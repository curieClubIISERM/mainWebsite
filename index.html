About Us

Curie Club, the chemistry club at IISER Mohali founded in 2011, aims to foster scientificinterest among students. Named in honor of Marie Curie, the club involves undergraduates, graduates, andfaculty inactivities such as scientific talks, project presentations, experiment demonstrations,and outreach programs. They also screen Nobel lectures and organize industrial visits to showcasechemistry'spracticalapplications. Every November, Curie Club celebrates 'Chem Week' with talks,interactive sessions, andvarious activities to commemorate the birth anniversaries of Sir CVRaman and Marie Curie. Videos of their events are available on their YouTube channel, aiming toinspire a broader scientific community.
